Photography: Space

This task here was to take a set of photographs documenting a space that I was familiar with or that means something to me. This was shortly after I moved out of home for the first time so I wanted to document something I noticed during this process.



When I returned home after I moved out, I noticed that my bedroom looked like it was in this strange stage of being half moved out. I'd taken a load of posters and photographs with me, to make my new room feel more homely, but in the process, I had left my old room with these left over marks of where things used to be. I realised that it made it feel like I had half of one home to go to, and half of another.
