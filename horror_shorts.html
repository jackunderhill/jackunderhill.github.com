Crushed Beaks - Horror Shorts

Myself and Gurpreet Panesar were approached to direct a music video for Chrushed Beaks' 'Horror Shorts', so we took this opportunity to see what we could do with the high speed (1000 frames per second) camera that was donated to the University.



The narrative follows the memories that are attached to that box of sentimental objects that everyone has, and what might happen to these if the relationship turns sour.
