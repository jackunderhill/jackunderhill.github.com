Your Own Lens

This print was created in response to an observation of a guy in front of me at a gig, near-continually taking photos and subsequently uploading them to his social networkings sites).



The only way to view the typography clearly is to look through the red viewfinder which filters out most of the information. Hopefully it makes the viewer realise life shouldn't be experienced through a 4 inch screen
